About Me

I'm a passionate junior developer currently in my third year at the Faculty of Computer Science (RAF) in Belgrade. My journey began at the Technical School "Nikola Tesla" where I studied Energy Engineering, which gave me a solid foundation in technical thinking and problem-solving. This background sparked my interest in technology and led me to pursue computer science.

As someone at the beginning of their development journey, I'm deeply committed to learning and growing in this field. I have a strong foundation in frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm actively expanding my skills in modern frameworks and backend development.

While I'm comfortable with frontend development, I'm working hard to strengthen my backend skills to become a well-rounded developer. I believe in the importance of understanding both sides of development, and I'm dedicated to achieving that balance through continuous learning and practice.

Currently, I'm developing CodeQuest – a learning platform that combines my passion for gaming with my desire to help others learn programming. This project represents my commitment to growth and my goal of creating meaningful solutions that can make a difference.

I'm eager to take on new challenges, learn from experienced developers, and contribute to projects that push me beyond my current capabilities. My goal is to grow from a junior developer into a skilled professional who can tackle complex problems and deliver quality solutions.
